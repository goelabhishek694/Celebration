At a spitting distance from Delhi’s tall glory – the Qutub Minar- stands ‘’The Dramz’’ – a venue to celebrate our parents’ unending love for each other.

Their Story

  • First Meeting

    June 1994

    Indian parents don’t usually throw surprise parties for their kids, but when they do it is called an arranged marriage. So, as was the norm those days, our parents too met for an arranged marriage at New Friends Colony. We don’t have clarity over who liked whom first as until this date it remains a hot topic.

  • First Date

    July 1994

    After a happy agreement on both sides, our parents were sent on their first date to Lake Badhkal (I know right?) where even though we don’t know what they did, I think we can reasonably let our personal imaginations run its own course. Our dad’s always been a romantic at heart so the location doesn’t surprise us. But what did, was the regular exchange of letters over their entire period of courtship, more about which in the next segment.

  • Courtship

    July 1994 - February 1995

    So as we were saying – letters. Yes, there was a generous exchange of letters. Yes, we have proof. No, we haven’t been able to read them. But yes, we have had a sneak-peak. NO, you are not getting one too. But yes, you are free to interrogate them in person. AND NO, do not mention we were the ones who told you!

    Fun Fact:On one of their dates, our mom almost hit a pedestrian while behind the wheel. It is one of the many stories our dad loves to tell.

  • Holy Matrimony

    February 24, 1995

    So finally, after astrology had its way, they were married on 24th February 1995 in a big fat Indian wedding (it is almost badass that there was gun-firing too). But do you know what? This was just the beginning!
